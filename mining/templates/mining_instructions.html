Bitcoin Mining Simulation Instructions

Step-by-Step Guide

To simulate the process of Bitcoin mining, follow these steps:

  1. Set Difficulty Level: Adjust the difficulty level for mining by setting the number of leading zeros required in the block hash.
  2. Claim Block Rewards: Begin by claiming the block reward. This simulates the reward that miners get for successfully mining a block.
  3. Create Transactions: Enter the details of the transactions you want to record on the block. Include sender, receiver, and the amount of Bitcoin to transfer.
  4. Verify Wallet Balance: Ensure that the sending address has enough Bitcoin to cover the transaction. Transactions will not be processed without sufficient balance.
  5. Find the Nonce: After finding the nonce that generates the correct number leading zeroes, click 'Mine Block' and successfully mine the block.
  6. Review the Blockchain: After mining the block, review the blockchain to see the list of all mined blocks, including their nonces, hashes, and transactions.
  7. Auto Mine Option: After completing round 3, the auto mine option becomes available, streamlining the mining process for subsequent blocks.

Follow these instructions to understand the role of miners and the intricacies of blockchain technology. Happy mining!

Game 4: Firm Reactor Instructions

Instructions: Earn tokens by correctly answering two questions in the first round, then embark on a multi-round price guessing game as you experience a market moving toward a long-run competitive equilibrium, with a few detours along the way. You’ll lose tokens for inaccurate guesses and the game ends when you run out of tokens.

Round 0:

You’re given a market demand, firm-level cost functions and the current market price. You must correctly calculate the profit-maximizing quantity and decide if this firm should enter the market. Earn 5 tokens for each question you answer correctly.

Guessing Rounds:

In the rounds that follow, you’ll guess the price adjustments as the market experiences a change to the number of firms in the market and demand shocks. Inaccurate guesses reduce your tokens, but you can replenish tokens with very accurate guesses.

Scoring:

Your score is equal to the number of rounds you can remain in the game before losing all of your tokens. See how high you can go!
